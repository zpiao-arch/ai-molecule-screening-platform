# Disclaimer

This project is a computational screening and workflow demonstration platform.

It does not provide medical advice, clinical decision support, treatment recommendations, diagnostic output, dosing guidance, toxicity prediction as a safety guarantee, or proof of biological efficacy.

Docking scores, proxy scores, generated molecules, known-drug matches, and dashboard summaries are prioritization signals for research planning only. They must be independently reviewed by qualified scientists and validated through appropriate biochemical, cell-based, toxicology, pharmacology, and regulatory workflows before any real-world use.

The project intentionally separates:

- computational screening;
- structure and descriptor readiness;
- known-control calibration;
- pose quality checks;
- retrospective benchmarks;
- experimental or clinical validation, which is outside this repository.

Do not use this repository to claim that a molecule is safe, effective, approved, or clinically useful.
